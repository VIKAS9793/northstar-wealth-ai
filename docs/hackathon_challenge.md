# Vision Statement

Transform wealth management from product-centric selling into goal-centric financial coaching by providing every customer with an AI-powered Digital Relationship Manager that combines financial planning, investor education, behavioral coaching, suitability assessment, financial resilience planning, and compliant wealth guidance.

---

# Problem Statement Reframed

Most wealth management platforms focus on recommending products.

Most customers do not understand:

* Mutual fund categories
* Asset allocation
* Alpha/Beta
* Expense ratios
* Market cycles
* Risk-adjusted returns
* Portfolio suitability
* Risk capacity

Customers think in terms of life goals, not financial products.

They ask:

* How can I buy a house?
* How much should I invest for retirement?
* How do I save for my child's education?
* Where should I invest my bonus?
* Is market volatility dangerous?
* Why is my SIP not growing fast enough?
* Should I stop my SIP during a market crash?
* My friend invested here, should I also invest?
* Why shouldn't I move to the top-performing fund?

The gap between financial products and customer understanding creates confusion, emotional decisions, poor discipline, panic redemptions, SIP discontinuation, and goal failures.

Our solution bridges this gap.

# AI Governance Engine

Every AI response passes through a compliance layer.

---

## Prohibited Statements

Never allow:

* Guaranteed returns
* Assured wealth creation
* Best fund claims
* Future return promises
* Certain outcomes

Examples:

❌ "This fund will generate 15% CAGR."

❌ "You will definitely achieve ₹1 crore."

---

## Allowed Statements

Examples:

✅ "This investment option may align with your stated goals and risk profile."

✅ "Historical performance does not guarantee future returns."

✅ "Projected outcomes are based on assumptions and may vary."

---

## Explainability Requirements

Every recommendation must explain:

* Why
* Assumptions
* Risks
* Alternatives
* Suitability rationale
